The Logic

The Simple Logic Behind Every Automation

Every automation, from the simplest reminder to the most complex multi-system workflow, follows four steps.

Trigger

Something happens. A new order lands. A form gets submitted. An invoice becomes overdue.

Rule

A decision is made. IF the order is over R5,000, THEN flag as high-priority.

Condition

A check runs. Is this a returning customer? Has this been paid already?

Action

The task gets done. Tag them in the CRM. Send the email. Alert the manager. Generate the PDF.

That's it. Once it's set up, it runs every time. Perfectly, 24/7, without coffee breaks.
