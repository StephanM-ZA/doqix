# Team — What's Included

**Version:** 1.0 · **Date:** 2026-04-15

## Included in Monthly Retainer

- **Up to 3 live workflows** — fully built, hosted, and maintained
- **Workflow builds** — covered by setup fee; no separate build quotes
- **Hosting & monitoring** — n8n on SA-based infrastructure
- **Uptime monitoring** with proactive alerts
- **Maintenance & updates** — app version updates, connection fixes, tweaks
- **POPIA-compliant data handling**
- **Priority support** — email + WhatsApp business line, 24-hour response SLA
- **Ownership of your workflows** — if we part ways, you keep everything
- **Month-to-month cancellation**

- **Up to 3 workflows** (add more at +R650/mo each)
- **Standard integrations** — common SaaS; legacy/on-prem is Enterprise
- **No dedicated account manager** (Business tier)
- **No monthly strategy call** (Business tier)
