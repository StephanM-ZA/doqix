## Core Value Claim

## Break-Even Math

| Inputs | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Blended team hourly cost | R200/hr (salary loaded) |
| Team monthly cost | R2,500 |
| **Break-even** | **~12.5 hours/month** saved across the team |

Most Team clients save **30–80 hours/month** — 2.5–6x break-even.

## Payback Period (for the customer)

At typical savings (~R10,000/mo value):
- **Month 1 (launch offer):** Pay R1,250 + R1,500 setup = R2,750. Save R10,000. **Net: +R7,250.**
- **Month 2 onward:** Pay R2,500, save R10,000+. **Net: +R7,500/mo ongoing.**

Full payback of setup + first month within **week 1** of workflows going live.

## Multi-Workflow Compounding

The Team tier is where the real compounding kicks in:

- **One workflow** saves hours in one place
- **Three workflows** remove the three biggest daily friction points — team starts to feel like the business actually runs smoothly
- That feeling is what makes the upsell conversation easy ("what else can we automate?")
