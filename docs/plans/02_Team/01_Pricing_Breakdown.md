## Add-On Math (worked example)

Team client who adds 3 extra workflows (6 total):

```
R2,500  (base, 3 workflows)
+ R650  (workflow #4)
+ R650  (workflow #5)
+ R650  (workflow #6)
= R4,450/mo

→ At 6 workflows, Business (R5,500/mo) is the natural upgrade
  — only R1,050 more for included training, monthly strategy call, and dedicated support.
```

| Line | Amount |
|------|--------|
