# Team — Target Customer

**Version:** 1.0 · **Date:** 2026-04-15

---

## Who This Is For

- **Small businesses with 2–15 staff**
- **Growing practices** (accounting, legal, creative agencies, consulting)
- **Ecommerce stores** with inventory, orders, and customer comms to juggle
- **Service businesses** (trades, installers, maintenance) managing quotes, schedules, invoicing
- **Solo clients graduating** from Solo with 2–3 workflows in play

### Buyer profile
- **Team size:** 2–15
- **Monthly tool budget:** R2,000–R6,000
- **Pain threshold:** 10+ team hours/week lost to repetitive tasks
- **Tech comfort:** Uses multiple SaaS tools; feels the pain of things not talking to each other
- **Buying signal:** "We've got three things that keep breaking/slipping — can you automate them?"

---

## Who This Is NOT For

- Solopreneurs with one task — **Solo** is cheaper and enough to start
- Companies with 15+ staff spanning finance/sales/ops — **Business** (more workflows, strategy call, included training)
- Teams needing SSO, audit trails, legacy integration — **Enterprise**
- Anyone with 7+ workflows on day one (economics tilt to Business)

---

## Qualifying Questions

Ask on intake:

1. How many people on the team today?
2. Which three things would you automate first if you could?
3. How many tools are involved per process?
4. How much time does the team lose to this kind of work in a week?
5. Are any of these tools legacy or on-premise?

**Green light:** 2–15 staff, 3 clear workflows, common SaaS stack, 10+ hrs/wk lost.
**Redirect to Solo:** 1 person or 1 workflow only.
**Redirect to Business:** 15+ staff, multi-department, wants strategy call.
**Redirect to Enterprise:** Legacy systems, SSO, compliance, unlimited scope needed.

---

## Natural Upgrade Path

```
Team (R2,500, 3 workflows)
  └─ "The finance one works great — now we want the same for sales ops and customer support."
      └─ Option A: Add +R650/mo per extra workflow
      └─ Option B (at 6 workflows): Upgrade to Business (R5,500 for 6 + strategy call + included training)
```

**Rule of thumb:** Once a Team client passes 4 workflows OR asks for ongoing advice on what to automate next, pitch Business.
