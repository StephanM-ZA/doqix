# Solo — Target Customer

**Version:** 1.0 · **Date:** 2026-04-15

---

## Who This Is For

- **Solopreneurs** running their own business with no employees
- **Freelancers & consultants** whose admin is eating their billable hours
- **Side-hustle founders** bootstrapping toward full-time
- **Micro-practices** (bookkeepers, coaches, solo legal/medical) drowning in repetitive admin

### Buyer profile
- **Team size:** 1 (possibly a VA or part-time helper)
- **Monthly tool budget:** R500–R2,000
- **Pain threshold:** Already losing 5+ hours/week to repetitive tasks
- **Tech comfort:** Uses SaaS (Xero, Google, Shopify) but not a "builder"
- **Buying signal:** "I just need this one thing to stop eating my time"

---

## Who This Is NOT For

- Teams of 2+ — **Team plan** is cheaper per workflow and includes priority support
- Anyone needing more than 1 workflow on day one (start with Team)
- Regulated industries that need audit trails / SSO (Enterprise)
- Customers who want a monthly strategy call (Business)
- Anyone who needs workflows across multiple departments

---

## Qualifying Questions

Ask these on the intake call / form:

1. Are you running this business solo, or do you have a team?
2. What's the one task you'd automate first if you could?
3. How many hours a week do you spend on it today?
4. What tools are involved? (Looking for common SaaS — Xero, Sheets, Gmail, Shopify, WhatsApp)
5. What's your monthly tool/software budget right now?

**Green light:** Solo, clear single-workflow pain, 5+ hours/wk lost, common SaaS, R1K+ budget.
**Redirect to Team:** 2+ people involved, or multiple workflows needed from day one.
**Redirect to Business:** Multiple departments, 15+ staff.

---

## Natural Upgrade Path

```
Solo (R999, 1 workflow)
  └─ After 1–2 months: "This is saving me hours. Can you automate my lead follow-ups too?"
      └─ Option A: Add +R750/mo per extra workflow
      └─ Option B (at 3+ workflows): Upgrade to Team (R2,500 for 3)
```

**Rule of thumb:** Once a Solo client hits 2+ workflows, pitch the Team upgrade — it's cheaper and they get priority support.
