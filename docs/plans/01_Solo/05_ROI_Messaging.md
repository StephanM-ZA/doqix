## Break-Even Math

| Inputs | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Your effective hourly rate | R150/hr (conservative for solo) |
| Solo monthly cost | R999 |

## Anti-Objection: "Is It Really Worth It?"

Reframe every Solo conversation around the question:

> **What's your time actually worth — and what would you rather be doing with it?**

If the answer is "billable client work," "growing the business," or "not working weekends," R999/mo is almost always a yes.
