# Enterprise — Partnership Sub-Tier

**From R400,000 setup + R65,000/mo**

---

## Who it's for

Organisations where automation is **core infrastructure** — regulated industries, high-volume operations, multi-entity groups, or anyone with 24-7 criticality.

---

## What's included

- **Unlimited workflow coverage** within contracted scope
- **Full integration spectrum** — standard SaaS, legacy, on-prem, custom databases, file-based systems
- **Dedicated solutions engineer** (named, with backup)
- **Email + WhatsApp + phone** support channels
- **1-hour first response** on P1 (24-7)
- **On-call SLA** for production-critical workflows
- **Monthly roadmap sessions** (60–90 min)
- **Quarterly business reviews** with leadership
- **SSO, audit trails, role-based access** as standard
- **Security review** included in setup
- **Change-request credit pool** — 20 hrs/month at reduced rate
- **Uptime commitment:** 99.9%
- Hosting options: SA cloud, dedicated infrastructure, or client-hosted per requirements
- Full documentation + runbooks + DR procedures
- Training for multiple stakeholder tiers (end-users + admins + execs)

---

## What's excluded (still billed separately)

- Net-new workflows outside agreed annual scope → quoted per CR
- Major refactors of existing workflows → quoted per CR
- Net-new system integrations (not contemplated at SOW time) → quoted
- Custom BI / reporting platforms → custom development quote
- Bespoke end-user portals/UIs → custom development quote
- Third-party API & platform costs → pass-through

## Sell it when...

- Regulated industry — financial services, healthcare, insurance, legal
- High volume (100K+ runs/month) or 24-7 criticality
- Legacy / on-prem integration is central to scope
- SSO + audit + compliance are non-negotiable
- Client explicitly wants a named team, not a queue
- Multi-entity, multi-region, or multi-year programme
- Client has capacity to truly partner (champion, budget, executive sponsor)

## Don't sell it when...

- Scope fits in 6–8 workflows → Growth is enough
- Budget won't support R1M+ year-one investment → Growth or Foundation
- Internal stakeholders aren't aligned — on-call + dedicated engineer only pays off with real partnership

## The Partnership Narrative

> "Partnership isn't a more expensive tier. It's a different operating model. You get a team that knows your business, not a ticket queue. We show up to your exec reviews. We build the 5-year roadmap with you. Your ops team spends more time on growth and less on fighting fires — because we're the ones holding the fire extinguisher."

This framing justifies the price gap from Growth. If it doesn't resonate, sell Growth.
