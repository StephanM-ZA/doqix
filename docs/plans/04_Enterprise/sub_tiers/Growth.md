# Enterprise — Growth Sub-Tier ⭐ *Recommended*

**From R180,000 setup + R35,000/mo**

> The sub-tier most enterprise clients land on. Presented as the recommended option in every proposal.

---

## Who it's for

Established mid-market businesses rolling out automation across multiple departments. Past the "trying it out" phase — committed to automation as an operating model.

---

## What's included

- **6–8 workflows** across departments
- **Standard + medium-complexity integrations** (OAuth, rate-limited APIs, moderate data transforms)
- **Priority support** — email + WhatsApp, 8-business-hour first response
- **Quarterly business review** (60–90 min with ops leadership)
- **Change-request credit pool** — 10 hrs/month at reduced rate; rolls over 1 quarter max
- **Uptime commitment:** 99.5%
- Hosting, monitoring, maintenance across all contracted workflows
- POPIA-compliant handling
- Full documentation package
- Initial training for stakeholder group

---

## What's excluded (or add-on)

- 24-7 on-call SLA → Partnership
- Phone support channel → Partnership
- Legacy / on-prem integration → Partnership (or scoped add-on with uplift)
- SSO / audit trails / RBAC → Partnership (or scoped add-on)
- Dedicated solutions engineer → Partnership
- Monthly roadmap sessions → Partnership (quarterly instead)

## Sell it when...

- 50–250 staff, established operations
- 6+ workflows scoped in discovery, across 2–4 departments
- Wants a real SLA but not 24-7 on-call
- Quarterly business review is genuinely useful to them
- Willing to commit to 12-month term

## Don't sell it when...

- Regulated industry demanding 1-hour response / on-call → Partnership
- Legacy / on-prem is central to the scope → Partnership
- Only 3 workflows needed → Foundation
- Client wants dedicated engineer → Partnership

---

## Why this is the "recommended" tier

- Price-anchored between Foundation and Partnership — middle option wins by default
- Scope (6–8 workflows) covers most real mid-market use cases
- SLA is strong enough to matter, light enough to deliver profitably
- Change-request credit pool signals flexibility without uncapping scope
