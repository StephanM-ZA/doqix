### FAQ (enterprise-specific)

**How long does an enterprise rollout take?**
Most engagements go live in 6–12 weeks after kickoff. Partnership sometimes runs 16+ weeks for multi-phase rollouts.

**Why the paid discovery sprint?**
Accurate quotes need accurate inputs. We map your processes, pressure-test assumptions, and come back with a proposal you can trust. Fee is credited against build if you proceed. It also filters tyre-kickers — protects both of us.

**Do you integrate with on-premise / legacy systems?**
Yes. SAP, Oracle, Syspro, Sage, custom WMS/POS, anything with an API, file export, or database. Partnership tier is built for it.

**What happens if we outgrow the tier we picked?**
We move you up, pro-rata, no penalty. That's the point of a managed service — it flexes with you.

**Can we bring this in-house later?**
Yes. You own the workflows. If you build an internal team and want to take over, we hand over with full docs and a transition period. No ransom, no drama.

**Where is our data hosted?**
Depends on your requirements. Default is cloud (SA region). For regulated sectors, we deploy in your environment or dedicated infrastructure.

**What's not included?**
New workflows beyond scope, major refactors, new integrations, after-hours emergency support (except Partnership), custom BI dashboards. Quoted separately and transparently.

**Can we terminate?**
Yes. 90 days' written notice. No lock-in clauses, no penalty clauses.
