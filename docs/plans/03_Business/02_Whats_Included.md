# Business — What's Included

**Version:** 1.0 · **Date:** 2026-04-15

---

## Included in Monthly Retainer

- **Up to 6 live workflows** — fully built, hosted, maintained
- **Workflow builds** — covered by setup fee
- **Hosting & monitoring** — n8n on SA-based infrastructure
- **Uptime monitoring** with proactive alerts
- **Maintenance & updates** across all 6 workflows
- **POPIA-compliant data handling**
- **Dedicated support** — named point of contact (Stephan or delegate)
- **Monthly strategy call** (60 min) — review, roadmap, new opportunities
- **Training sessions — included** (unlimited within reason)
- **Priority channels** — email + WhatsApp + scheduled calls
- **Ownership of your workflows**
- **Month-to-month cancellation**

| Item | Cost | Notes |

## Limits

- **Up to 6 workflows** (add more at +R500/mo each; 10+ → Enterprise conversation)
- **Standard integrations only** — common SaaS ecosystem
- **Shared infrastructure** — dedicated hosting is Enterprise tier
- **Business hours SLA** — 24-7 on-call is Enterprise

## Monthly Strategy Call — What It Covers

1. **Performance review** — how are the current workflows doing? Any drift?
2. **New automation opportunities** — what's bugging the team this month?
3. **Roadmap** — what we're building/adjusting next
4. **Q&A / training** — anyone need a refresher on anything?
5. **Account health check** — utilisation, margin, support quality

This call is a commitment both ways. Missing 2 in a row triggers a check-in to make sure the tier still fits.
