## The Strategic ROI (harder to quantify, easier to feel)

Beyond hours saved, Business clients report:
- **Fewer errors** — automated data flow eliminates transposition mistakes
- **Faster cycle times** — invoice-to-payment drops 3–7 days typical
- **Better staff retention** — people hired for skills actually use those skills
- **Scaling headroom** — can add 20–30% more volume without new hires
- **Visibility** — dashboards and alerts catch problems before customers do

These are hard to put a number on, but clients call them out consistently in reviews.
