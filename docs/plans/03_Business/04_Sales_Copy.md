## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "R5,500 is a big jump from Team." | "Six workflows vs three, dedicated support vs priority, training included vs R1,500/session, and a monthly strategy call that typically surfaces 2–3 new automations a year. The jump is proportional to the scope." |
| "Can we start on Team and upgrade?" | "Absolutely — many clients do. But if you already have 4+ processes in mind, the math flips: 4 extra workflows on Team (R2,500 + 4×R650 = R5,100) is almost Business pricing without the strategy call or training." |
| "What's the monthly strategy call actually for?" | "60 min with me. We review current workflows, surface new opportunities, update the roadmap. It's how Business clients save 30%+ more than Team clients — because we're constantly finding the next time-sink." |
| "We have some legacy systems — can you handle those?" | "Standard SaaS yes. Legacy/on-prem is Enterprise territory. Happy to do a quick audit to see which camp you're in." |
| "Do we need to use all 6 workflows?" | "No. We start with 3–4, usually add the rest over months 2–4 as we learn what's working and what else is painful." |
