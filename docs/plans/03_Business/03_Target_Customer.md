# Business — Target Customer

**Version:** 1.0 · **Date:** 2026-04-15

---

## Who This Is For

- **Growing SMEs with 15–50 staff**
- **Multi-department operations** — finance, sales, ops, support all affected
- **Businesses with recurring operational pain** that spans teams
- **Owners who want a thinking partner** on automation, not just a vendor
- **Established Team clients** graduating upward

### Buyer profile
- **Team size:** 15–50
- **Monthly tool budget:** R5,000–R15,000
- **Pain threshold:** 25+ team hours/week lost to manual work across departments
- **Decision maker:** Owner / MD / Ops Director / CFO
- **Tech comfort:** Runs real software stack (ERP, CRM, finance, comms), feels integration gaps
- **Buying signal:** "We need to get our operations under control before we scale further."

---

## Who This Is NOT For

- Teams under 15 or with single-department scope → **Team**
- Solopreneurs → **Solo**
- 50+ staff, complex stack, SSO, compliance, legacy systems → **Enterprise**
- Project-based buyers who want "just build us this one thing and leave" — our model is managed retainer

---

## Qualifying Questions

Ask on intake:

1. How many people in the business? Which departments?
2. Which processes are costing you the most across finance, sales, and ops?
3. What's your current tool stack? (Looking for CRM + finance + comms + maybe ERP)
4. Are you scaling — hiring, expanding, opening new branches?
5. Do you want us to actively propose new automations, or only build what you ask for?
6. Any regulatory requirements? Any on-prem systems?

**Green light:** 15–50 staff, 6 workflows across departments, standard SaaS stack, wants strategic input.
**Redirect to Team:** Single department focus, < 15 staff.
**Redirect to Enterprise:** 50+ staff, legacy/on-prem, SSO, compliance, unlimited scope needed.

---

## Natural Upgrade Path

```
Business (R5,500, 6 workflows + strategy call)
  └─ "We want to automate everything — finance, HR, customer ops, logistics."
      └─ Option A: Add workflows at +R500/mo each (up to ~10 before economics flip)
      └─ Option B (10+ workflows OR compliance/SSO need): Enterprise conversation
```

**Rule of thumb:** Once a Business client hits 10+ workflows, asks about SSO/audit trails, or starts talking about legacy/on-prem integration, start the Enterprise discovery sprint conversation.

---

## Why Business is the "Committed Client" Tier

- R2,500 setup + R5,500/mo monthly = meaningful financial commitment
- Monthly strategy call creates relationship depth — churn drops dramatically
- Included training builds internal champions who defend the tool
- Cross-departmental impact makes replacement genuinely painful (the good kind of switching cost)

These clients are the foundation of sustainable MRR.
