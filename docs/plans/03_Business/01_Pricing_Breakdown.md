## Add-On Math (worked example)

Business client who adds 4 extra workflows (10 total):

```
R5,500  (base, 6 workflows)
+ R500  (workflow #7)
+ R500  (workflow #8)
+ R500  (workflow #9)
+ R500  (workflow #10)
= R7,500/mo

→ At ~10+ workflows, the conversation shifts to Enterprise scoping
  (unlimited workflows within scope, dedicated account manager).
```
