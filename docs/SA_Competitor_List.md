## Key Takeaways

**Who to watch closest:** AgentK (same tools, same market), Autopilot (best brand voice), Fun Things (n8n + audit model)

**Market gaps nobody is filling:**
- Authentically South African voice (load-shedding, rand pricing, WhatsApp, POPIA)
- Visible pricing in ZAR on the website
- Owning the n8n self-hosted niche with authority
- Addressing job-loss anxiety directly
- Industry-specific use cases for SA verticals
- Real SA case studies with named businesses and numbers
