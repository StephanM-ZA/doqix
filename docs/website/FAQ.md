## Section 1 — Getting Started

### What does Do.Qix actually do?
We build and run business automations for you. You tell us what's eating your team's time — we design, build, host, and maintain the workflow that removes that work. You don't touch a line of code. We handle everything.

### How is this different from Zapier or Make?
Zapier and Make are DIY tools — you (or your team) build and maintain the automations yourself, pay per task, and learn the platform. Do.Qix is a **managed service** — we do the building, the hosting, the fixing, and the ongoing tweaks. Flat ZAR retainer, no per-task fees, and no team time spent learning a new platform.

### Do I need any technical knowledge?
None. We handle everything — design, building, testing, hosting, and ongoing management. If you can describe what you want to happen, we can build it.

### How do I know if automation is right for my business?
If your team is doing the same task more than 3 times a week, it's a candidate. Book a 15-minute free call — we'll map where your time is going and tell you honestly whether we can help. If we can't, we'll often point you to someone who can.

### What's the first step?
Book a free call. No sales pitch, no pressure. We listen, ask questions, and either propose a workflow or tell you it's not worth the effort. Takes 15–30 minutes.

## Section 2 — Pricing & Plans

### What does it cost?
Four plans, all in South African rands, all month-to-month:
- **Solo** — R999/mo, no setup fee (1 workflow)
- **Team** — R2,500/mo + R1,500 setup (up to 3 workflows) ⭐ Most popular
- **Business** — R5,500/mo + R2,500 setup (up to 6 workflows)
- **Enterprise** — Custom quote (unlimited scoped workflows, from R15,000/mo)

Full breakdown: see the Pricing section.

### Why no per-user or per-task pricing?
A workflow runs whether 2 people benefit or 50 — charging per user punishes growth. Per-task fees explode at volume. We charge a flat retainer because that's what makes sense for automation-as-a-service.

### Is there a free trial?
No free trial, but launch pricing is **50% off month 1** on every tier. On Solo that's R500 to prove the concept. If it doesn't save you more than that in month one, tell us — we'll make it right.

### What's included in the setup fee?
Scoping. We map your current processes, agree on what we're building, and design the workflows. Solo skips the setup fee because one workflow is quick to scope. Team and Business charge modest setup fees because more workflows need more upfront design. Enterprise setup is quoted per engagement.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Month-to-month on every plan. No lock-in, no penalties, no exit fees (except Enterprise — 90 days' notice on the 12-month contract). We keep clients because we deliver, not because of contracts.

### What if I outgrow my plan?
Upgrade anytime, pro-rata. Most clients start on Solo or Team and move up as they add workflows. The pricing is designed so the upgrade is obvious — e.g. once a Solo client has 3 workflows (R999 + 2×R750 = R2,499), moving to Team (R2,500 for 3 included + priority support) is a no-brainer.

### Do you offer annual discounts?
We don't publish one. Enterprise multi-year commitments can negotiate up to 10% monthly discount. For Solo/Team/Business, month-to-month at the published rate is the deal — we'd rather you leave when we stop delivering than be locked in.

### Are prices VAT-inclusive?
Prices are quoted exclusive of VAT. Where VAT applies, it's added at invoicing.

## Section 3 — What Gets Built & How

### What counts as one "workflow"?
A workflow is one connected sequence that starts with a trigger and ends with an outcome. Example: "New Shopify order → create Xero invoice → send WhatsApp confirmation → add to Google Sheet" is **one workflow** with multiple steps. We don't count every step; we count the end-to-end process.

### Can you give me examples of workflows you've built?
Common ones:
- **Invoicing on autopilot** — quote accepted → Xero invoice → payment reminders → receipts
- **Lead capture** — form submission → CRM → assigned salesperson → welcome email
- **Order processing** — online order → stock update → fulfilment brief → customer SMS
- **Inbox triage** — incoming email → classification → routing to right person/queue
- **Weekly reporting** — data pull from 3 systems → summary sheet → auto-send to team
- **Booking reminders** — calendar event → WhatsApp reminder 24hr before → follow-up if missed

### How long does a workflow take to build?
- **Simple** (1–2 systems, happy path): 3–7 working days
- **Medium** (3–4 systems, branching, error handling): 1–3 weeks
- **Complex** (legacy integration, edge cases, compliance): 3–6 weeks
- **Enterprise multi-workflow rollout:** 6–12 weeks typical

We give you a clear timeline before we start. No "we'll see."

### What apps and tools do you work with?
Over 400. The most common:
Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Xero, QuickBooks, Sage, Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Slack, WhatsApp Business, Shopify, WooCommerce, Takealot, Airtable, Notion, BambooHR, Zendesk, Intercom, Eventbrite, DocuSign, Trello, Asana, Jira, WordPress, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Stripe, Yoco, Ozow, Peach Payments, Typeform, Jotform, Twilio, Buffer, Meta suite, LinkedIn, Calendly.

**If your app has an API, we can almost always connect it.** For legacy/on-prem systems without APIs, see Enterprise.

### What if my app isn't on that list?
Tell us. Most tools have an API even if it's not obvious. We check during the free call — if we can't integrate, we'll say so upfront.

### Can you integrate with legacy or on-premise systems?
Yes, on the **Enterprise** tier. Think SAP, Oracle, Syspro, Sage 200/300, custom warehouse or point-of-sale systems, file-based integrations (CSV, SFTP), or on-prem databases. Our Partnership sub-tier is built for exactly this.

### What happens if the workflow breaks?
Our monitoring catches most issues before you do. You'll get a notification, we diagnose, we fix — included in your retainer. For urgent issues, priority support channels (WhatsApp on Team+, phone on Enterprise Partnership) escalate faster.

### Can I change a workflow after it's built?
Yes. Minor tweaks (<2 hours) are included in your retainer every month. Bigger changes are quoted as a change request. On Enterprise Growth+, there's a change-request credit pool built in.

### Do I own the workflows you build?
Yes. You own them completely. If we part ways, we hand over the full workflow definitions, documentation, and access — no ransom, no drama. That's our no-lock-in promise.

## Section 4 — Support & SLA

### What's your support SLA?
Depends on your plan:
- **Solo** — Email, 48-hour first response, business hours
- **Team** — Email + WhatsApp, 24-hour first response, business hours
- **Business** — Dedicated contact + WhatsApp, 8-business-hour first response, monthly strategy call
- **Enterprise Foundation** — Email, 2-business-day response
- **Enterprise Growth** — Email + WhatsApp, 8-business-hour response, quarterly reviews
- **Enterprise Partnership** — Email + WhatsApp + phone, 1-hour response, 24-7 on-call

### What counts as business hours?
Monday to Friday, 08:00–17:00 SAST (Business extends to 18:00). Enterprise Partnership is 24-7 for P1 issues.

### What if something breaks outside business hours?
- **Solo / Team / Business:** Best-effort response next business day, or R500/hr emergency rate if you need us out of hours
- **Enterprise Partnership:** 24-7 on-call included for P1 production issues

### What's a P1 issue vs a P2/P3?
- **P1** — Production workflow is down or causing data loss. Everything else stops until it's fixed.
- **P2** — Degraded but working (wrong output, delayed execution, intermittent errors). Fix within SLA.
- **P3** — Minor issue, feature request, non-urgent change. Handled in normal queue.

### Is there a chat / helpdesk / ticketing system?
Email + WhatsApp is the primary channel. We keep it simple — no ticketing bureaucracy. Enterprise engagements can set up a shared Slack/Teams channel or formal ticketing if required.

### Do you train my team?
- **Solo / Team** — Training sessions available at R1,500/session as needed
- **Business** — Training included (unlimited within reason)
- **Enterprise** — Initial onboarding included; ongoing training bundled into engagement

## Section 5 — Security, Data & Compliance

### Where does my data live?
Default: SA-based cloud infrastructure (Hetzner SA region). For regulated industries or specific requirements, Enterprise engagements can deploy to client-hosted environments, dedicated infrastructure, or specific regions.

### Is my data safe?
Yes. We self-host your automations on infrastructure we control. We don't use multi-tenant SaaS platforms that could leak data between clients. All connections are encrypted in transit; credentials are stored using industry-standard secret management.

### Are you POPIA compliant?
Yes. We treat your data as restricted, minimise data handling to what's needed for the workflow, maintain audit logs, and don't share client data with third parties. For specific POPIA responsibility allocation (responsible party vs operator), we sign a Data Processing Agreement on request — standard on Enterprise.

### Do you work in regulated industries?
Yes — financial services, insurance, healthcare, legal. These typically land on Enterprise Partnership where we handle sector-specific compliance (PCI for payments, sector guidelines for health, etc.). Required controls (SSO, audit trails, RBAC, security review) are built into the tier.

### Can I get SSO / audit trails / role-based access?
Yes, standard on Enterprise Partnership; available as an add-on on Enterprise Growth. Not configurable on Solo/Team/Business tiers — those are for businesses where the workflow owner = the admin.

### What happens to my data if we part ways?
You get everything back — workflow definitions, any data we've stored on your behalf, full documentation. We delete our copies within 30 days of termination unless you ask us to keep them longer.

### Do you sign NDAs?
Yes. Our standard MSA includes mutual confidentiality. We'll sign your NDA if required. For Enterprise engagements, NDAs are typically signed before the discovery sprint.

### Can I see your security documentation?
Yes — `docs/security/Security_Implementation_Guide.md` and `docs/security/n8n_Security_Recommendations.md` describe our approach. Enterprise clients get a formal security review as part of engagement.

## Section 6 — Process & Engagement

### What does the engagement look like?
1. **Free call** (15–30 min) — we listen, you describe the pain
2. **Proposal** (for Solo/Team/Business) or **paid discovery sprint** (for Enterprise, 1–2 weeks)
3. **Kickoff** — typically within a week of sign-up
4. **Build** — weekly demos so you see progress
5. **Pilot** — one workflow live first to validate
6. **Rollout** — remaining workflows phased
7. **Operate** — we run it; you live your life

### How long from first call to live workflow?
- **Solo:** Typically 1–2 weeks
- **Team:** 2–4 weeks (3 workflows phased)
- **Business:** 4–8 weeks (6 workflows phased over first 2 months)
- **Enterprise:** 6–12 weeks after discovery, depending on scope

### Will I see progress along the way?
Yes. Weekly demos on Team and above; regular check-ins on Solo. You see exactly what we're building before it goes live.

### What if I don't like what you've built?
We iterate until it's right — that's what the pilot phase is for. If the direction is fundamentally wrong, we pause and re-scope. We'd rather build the right thing twice than the wrong thing once.

### Can I start small and expand later?
Yes — that's the whole point of month-to-month. Most clients start on Solo or Team with one priority workflow, see value, then add more over time.

### Do you work with other automation partners / consultants?
Yes — we co-exist with in-house dev teams, IT departments, and other vendors. We're happy to pick up what others have started (or abandoned) and take it over.

## Section 7 — Enterprise & Complex Engagements

### Why do you charge for Enterprise discovery?
Accurate quotes need accurate inputs. In 1–2 weeks we map your actual processes, pressure-test assumptions, and come back with a proposal you can trust. The fee (R15K–R30K) is credited against the build if you proceed. It also filters out tyre-kickers.

### What's in the Enterprise proposal?
Three options — **Foundation** (from R80K setup + R15K/mo), **Growth** (from R180K + R35K, recommended), **Partnership** (from R400K + R65K) — with scope, timeline, SLA, and commercial terms for each.

### Can Enterprise be month-to-month?
Enterprise is 12-month minimum term with monthly invoicing and 90 days' written termination notice. Lighter than most enterprise contracts, stricter than our Solo/Team/Business offering — it's the trade-off for the scope.

### Do you do implementation only (no ongoing retainer)?
Our model is **managed service**, not project-based. We can do one-off builds if you insist, but pricing and terms are different, and it's not our focus.

### Can we bring the workflows in-house after you've built them?
Yes. You own everything. If you build an internal team and want to take over, we hand over with full documentation and a transition period. Some clients do this after 12–24 months; we're supportive of it.
