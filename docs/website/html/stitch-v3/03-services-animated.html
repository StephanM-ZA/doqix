The Simple Logic Behind Every Automation

Every automation — from the simplest reminder to the most complex multi-system workflow — follows four steps:

bolt

Trigger

Something happens.

A new order lands. A form gets submitted. An invoice becomes overdue.

account_tree

Rule

A decision is made.

IF the order is over R5,000, THEN flag as high-priority.

alt_route

Condition

A check runs.

Is this a returning customer? Has this been paid already?

smart_toy

Action

The task gets done.

Tag them in the CRM. Send the email. Alert the manager. Generate the PDF.

That's it. Once it's set up, it runs every time — perfectly, 24/7, without coffee breaks.

Our 5-Step Plan

We don't just throw tech at your problems. We follow a structured approach — because the biggest reason automation fails isn't the technology. It's skipping the prep.

01

Identify

We find the repetitive, rule-based, high-volume tasks. We talk to the people who actually do the work — they always know where the pain is.

02

Map

We chart your current process — every step, every handoff, every bottleneck. You can't automate what you can't see. And you shouldn't automate a broken process.

03

Start Small

We pick one process for a quick win. You see results fast, your team builds confidence, and we build from there.

04

Test & Refine

We stress-test everything. We deliberately try to break it. We check edge cases and make sure it's rock solid before it goes live.

05

Monitor & Optimise

Your automations go live but we don't walk away. We track performance, flag issues, and fine-tune as your business evolves.
