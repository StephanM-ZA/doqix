2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use analytics tools such as Google Analytics, and we do not track your behaviour across other websites.

However, certain external services used by our website may collect limited technical data:

  • GitHub Pages (hosts our website): May log your IP address and browser type in standard server logs. GitHub's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy statement.
  • Google Fonts (provides our typeface): Your browser connects to Google's servers to load fonts. This may expose your IP address to Google.
  • Tailwind CDN (provides styling): Your browser connects to Tailwind's CDN servers. This may expose your IP address.

We do not have access to any data collected by these third-party services.

2.3 Cookies

Our website does not set cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. The only local storage we use is:

  • sessionStorage (exit-intent popup): A flag to ensure our exit-intent popup only appears once per browser session. This data is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab and is never sent to our servers.

4. How We Process and Store Your Data

4.1 Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form:

  1. Your data is sent directly from your browser to our self-hosted n8n automation platform via an encrypted HTTPS connection.
  2. n8n generates a notification email to our team.
  3. Your submission is stored on our self-hosted infrastructure.
4.2 Infrastructure
  • All form data and client data is processed and stored on self-hosted infrastructure based in South Africa.
  • We use n8n, an open-source automation platform, which we manage and host ourselves.
  • We do not use third-party cloud CRM or marketing platforms to store your enquiry data.

5. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Service delivery: If you become a client, we connect your chosen business applications (e.g., Xero, Google Workspace, Slack) as part of delivering your automation workflows. Data shared with these applications is governed by their respective privacy policies and your existing agreements with them.
  • Legal obligation: If required by South African law, a court order, or the Information Regulator.
  • Professional advisers: Our accountants or legal advisers, bound by professional confidentiality.

6. International Data Transfers

Your personal information (form submissions, client data) is stored and processed on South African infrastructure. We do not transfer your personal data to servers outside South Africa.

However, our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which serves static files from servers that may be located outside South Africa (including the United States). No personal data is stored on GitHub Pages. Your form submissions go directly from your browser to our South African servers, not through GitHub.

When your browser loads our website, it also connects to:

  • Google Fonts (United States) to load typefaces
  • Tailwind CDN (United States) to load stylesheets

These connections may expose your IP address to US-based servers. We do not control how these providers handle that information.

If any cross-border transfer of personal data becomes necessary in future, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with POPIA Section 72, and will update this policy accordingly.

8. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  1. Access your personal information we hold.
  2. Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  3. Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).
  4. Object to the processing of your information on reasonable grounds.
  5. Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  6. Request data portability in a reasonable, machine-readable format.
  7. Not be subject to automated decision-making that produces legal effects. We do not use automated decision-making for any purpose that affects your rights.
How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at:

  • Email: hello@digitaloperations.co.za
  • Subject line: "POPIA Data Request"

There is no fee for exercising your rights, unless a request is manifestly unfounded or excessive.

9. Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Our measures include:

  • Encryption in transit: All data submitted through our website is transmitted via HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • Self-hosted infrastructure: Your data is stored on servers we manage, not on shared third-party cloud platforms.
  • Access control: Only authorised team members can access client data.
  • Regular updates: We keep our software and systems up to date with security patches.

No system is completely secure. If you become aware of any security concerns, please contact us immediately at hello@digitaloperations.co.za.

10. Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach that compromises your personal information, we will:

  1. Notify the Information Regulator as soon as reasonably possible, as required by POPIA Section 22.
  2. Notify you as soon as reasonably possible, providing details of what happened, what information was affected, and what steps we are taking.
  3. Take immediate steps to contain the breach and prevent further compromise.
