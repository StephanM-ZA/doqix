Identity

Visual Identity

Premium dark with electric energy. Near-black canvas, vivid teal accents, warm amber secondary. Confident and alive, never corporate or cold. Low density, generous whitespace, clean hierarchy through contrast and scale.

Font

Inter is the only typeface. All weights (400, 500, 600, 700, 900). No secondary fonts. Loaded from Google Fonts.

Icons

Heroicons (inline SVG). Outline style by default, solid for emphasis. Base class: .hi. Source: heroicons-master/optimized/24/

Tone

Direct, practical, South African. No em dashes. No fluff. Periods for new thoughts, commas for soft connections, colons for elaboration.

Mobile Behaviour (max 640px)

Sections centre-align text. Labels auto-centre. Section padding reduces (py-24 becomes 3rem). List items stay left-aligned within centred containers. Hamburger replaces nav links. CTA button hidden from header (shown in mobile menu).
