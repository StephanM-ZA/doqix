Continuity of VoltIQ depends on upstream manufacturer portals remaining available. Outages, rate limits, or breaking API changes on the upstream side are excluded from our uptime target. We will work to restore service as quickly as the upstream allows but accept no liability for losses resulting from upstream disruption.

You remain solely responsible for any installation, repair, or commercial decision made on the basis of VoltIQ output.

Automated issue detection is best-effort. False positives and missed events can occur, particularly during firmware updates, extreme weather, or upstream API issues. We continuously improve detection logic but make no warranty of completeness or accuracy.

SocialIQ

SocialIQ is an automated social media writer for SA businesses. It scrapes your industry sources, drafts 15 posts a week in your brand voice, and queues everything in a dashboard for you to approve before publishing.

SocialIQ pricing starts at R499 per month, in ZAR, billed monthly in advance. Final price scales with two dimensions: the social channels you choose and the posting cadence agreed at onboarding. Month-to-month, no long-term contract.

How pricing scales

The R499 floor reflects a baseline scope. Final monthly price depends on:

  • Number of social channels: each channel adds drafting and platform-appropriate formatting work. WhatsApp teaser and promo overlays attached to other channels are included, not counted as a separate channel.
  • Posting cadence: how many drafts per channel per week.

Scope, channels, cadence, and final price are confirmed in your engagement letter at onboarding before the first billing cycle. We don't lock you into a tier you didn't agree to.

What's included at every tier
  • Research engine: scrapes RSS feeds and other industry sources you configure, scores items for relevance, and surfaces the best material for the writer.
  • Brand-voice configuration: tone, vocabulary, do-not-say lists, and target audiences are configured once in your dashboard and apply to every draft.
  • Weekly preview: the next cycle's content lands in the dashboard for you to edit, approve, or kill before anything publishes.
  • Image generation: visuals are generated alongside each post; you can swap them in the dashboard.
  • Feedback loop: every approval, edit, and rejection sharpens the next round. Drafts sound more like you over time, not less.
What's not included
  • Publishing to platforms: SocialIQ produces ready-to-post drafts. You connect your own LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook accounts (or copy-paste into your scheduler). We do not store credentials for the destination platforms.
  • Paid media management: SocialIQ writes the organic content; ad spend, targeting, and campaign management are not part of this product.
  • Strategic consulting: SocialIQ executes a content cadence; messaging strategy and brand positioning are out of scope (we offer that separately under Automation Services).
AI-generated drafts are advisory

Every post is AI-generated and queued for human review. We expect you to read each draft before approving. SocialIQ's role is to remove the blank-page problem; final content responsibility (accuracy, claims, regulatory compliance for your industry) rests with the approver.

Data sources

You configure the research sources (RSS feeds, blogs, public APIs). SocialIQ does not scrape closed platforms or anything that requires logged-in access without your explicit setup. Continuity depends on those upstream sources remaining available; outages on the source side are excluded from our uptime target.

Cancellation

You may cancel at any time. On cancellation:

  • Drafting stops at the end of the current billing cycle.
  • Your dashboard remains read-only for a 30-day data export window. After that, your SocialIQ data is permanently deleted.
  • Cancellation does not affect your social accounts or anything you have already published.

The general cancellation terms in our Terms and Conditions apply alongside the above.
